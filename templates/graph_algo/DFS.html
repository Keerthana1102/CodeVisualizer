Depth-First Search

DFS is a graph traversal algorithm. First the starting vertex is selected and it further moves ontoone of its neighbours perform dfs on that neighbour and comes back to move onto next neighbour until no vertex has unvisited neighbours.The steps are:

  1. Take a stack
  2. Push startvertex into stack
  3. Pop the stack and push the neighbours of popped vertex that are not visited yet one by one
  4. Continue until stack is empty

Complexity:
Best Average Worst Memory
O(1) O(V+E) O(V+E) O(V)
